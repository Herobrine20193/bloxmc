Rules

We want to keep BloxMC a fun and fair place for everyone, so please follow these rules.

1. No Hacking or Exploiting – Play fair, no unfair advantages.
2. Respect Others – Treat fellow players and staff with kindness.
3. No Griefing or Stealing – Respect other players’ builds and loot.
4. No AFK Farming – Play actively and fairly.
5. No Spamming or Advertising – Keep chat friendly and clean.
6. No Scamming – Be honest in trades and deals.
7. Listen to Staff – Their decisions are final, but you can appeal if needed.

Survival GameMode

Classic Minecraft survival with a dynamic economy, player shops, and rank upgrades.

Dive into the classic Minecraft survival experience on BloxMC, now enhanced with an active player-driven economy and a feature-rich shop system. Gather resources, build your dream base, and sell items to other players or through the server shop to grow your wealth. Use your earnings to purchase powerful ranks that offer gameplay perks and convenience. With a balanced mix of challenge and progression, our Survival mode is perfect for both casual builders and seasoned adventurers.

LifeSteal GameMode

A high-stakes PvP survival mode where every kill makes you stronger – with crates, voting, and a robust economy system.

Lifesteal on BloxMC takes survival to the next level, blending intense PvP with strategic progression. In this competitive mode, each player kill steals a heart from your opponent, increasing your maximum health and dominance. Alongside the lifesteal mechanic, enjoy a full-featured economy with shops, item selling, and crate rewards. Vote daily to earn points and use them in the Points Shop to buy crate keys or other valuable items. Want an edge? Premium ranks and points are also available for purchase to supercharge your gameplay. Whether you're climbing the leaderboard or collecting loot, Lifesteal offers an adrenaline-pumping survival experience
